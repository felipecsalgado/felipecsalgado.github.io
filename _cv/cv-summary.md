## Research Specializations and Technical Competencies

### Physics Research Frameworks
* **Strong-Field Quantum Electrodynamics (QED):** Research includes non-perturbative Breit-Wheeler pair production, vacuum pair-creation, Compton scattering, and radiation reaction theories.
* **Laser-Plasma Interactions:** Execution of laser wakefield acceleration (LWFA) and electron-plasma acceleration experiments.
* **High-Power Laser Facility Operations:** Experimental research conducted at international high-power laser facilities, specifically JETi200, CALA, and FACET-II (SLAC).
* **Particle and Radiation Detection Systems:** Design, numerical simulation, and characterization of single-particle detectors, tracking screens, Cherenkov calorimeters, and particle spectrometers.

### Optics and Photonics Competencies
* **Laser Beamline Diagnostics:** Execution of Laser-Induced Damage Threshold (LIDT) measurements for high-power laser beamline optimization.
* **Optical and Optomechanical Engineering:** Development of precision optical imaging systems, optomechanical assemblies, and experimental components using Computer-Aided Design (CAD) via Autodesk Inventor.
* **Fiber Optics and Guided Wave Modeling:** Simulation of photonic modes in photonic crystal fibers (PCF) and directional couplers using COMSOL and RSOFT.
* **Optoelectronics and Sensor Instrumentation:** Engineering of Optical Time-Domain Reflectometers (OTDR), Raman Distributed Temperature Sensing (DTS) systems, Fiber Bragg Grating (FBG) sensor networks, and semiconductor laser diodes or optical amplifiers with Proportional-Integral-Derivative (PID) controllers.
